Young Artis Experience

Concept development

Made in 2020

ARTIS is a meeting place where they bring nature up close in an inventive way and discuss how humans value, understand and treat nature. More than 1.4 million people visit the ARTIS Park and Micropia every year.

Briefing

Artis wants to make signage for children a much more important part of the Artis experience. The aim is for the child to guide the way through the park, not the parent. Create a total concept, specifically aimed at the young, curious animal friend.
